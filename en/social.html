Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate Social Responsibility is defined as a voluntary commitment, including economic, social and environmental criteria and actions in our corporate practice.

"MESMER" endeavors to become a leader in corporate citizenship, creating a sustainable future for younger generations. Our environmental commitment is to reduce the global impact of our production processes, which allows both "MESMER" and our customers to work more effectively through improved health, education and environmental protection.

"MESMER" strives to create working conditions that minimize the risk to the health of all employees. We do our best to solve problems related to the environmental and health impacts of our technological processes by reducing waste and emissions, as well as maintaining work conditions to prevent accidents and injuries. In addition to supporting the development of natural sources, we provide direct social benefits to a large number of people in need.

Social responsibility of "MESMER" include:
• protection of the environment and natural resources.
• promotion of standardization and quality culture.
• supporting communities and social welfare through jobs creation.
• development of a culture of cooperation and partnership.

Sustainable Development

"MESMER" tries to contribute to meeting 7 of 17 United Nation (UN) sustainable development goals. We play an active role in caring for our planet now and in the future, protecting the environment.

No. 6 CLEAN WATER AND SANITATION
No. 7 AFFORDABLE AND CLEAN ENERGY
No. 8 DECENT WORK AND ECONOMIC GROWTH
No. 9 INDUSTRY, INNOVATION AND INFRASTRUCTURE
No. 11 SUSTAINABLE CITIES AND COMMUNITIES
No. 13 CLIMATE ACTION
No. 17 PARTNERSHIP TO ACHIEVE THE SET GOALS

Sustainability is also one of the main activities of "MESMER" to create strategies and solve the problem of marine pollution, which is valuable for our customers.
