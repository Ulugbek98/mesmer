Gulistan is a flourishing town!
«MESMER-EAST» LLC, with the financing of the Islamic Development Bank, has completed the construction and reconstruction of part of the sewer systems in Gulistan under the contract SSIP/ICB/MC/W-1.2-4 «Reconstruction and Expansion of Sewerage Systems of Gulistan, Shirin and Yangiyеr of Syrdarya Region Project».
22.924 km of pipelines were laid, 3 sewage pumping stations with a capacity of 4.7/13.5/5.9 l/s were installed. 1225 households were connected to the sewer network.
In addition, the following results of social significance were achieved:

1. Sanitary safety. Outdated or insufficient sewerage systems in the city of Gulistan were a source of water and land pollution, which posed a threat to the health of the town residents.The expansion and modernization of the system, implemented by MESMER-EAST LLC, made it possible to effectively manage wastewater, minimize the risk of contamination and ensure clean environment.

2. Environmental improvement. The installation of modern sewerage systems contributed to the conservation of water resources, the maintenance of biodiversity and the prevention of environmental damage.

3. Economic benefit. Improved sewer systems have reduced system maintenance costs by reducing accidents and water loss.
