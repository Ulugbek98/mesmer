WE ARE IN SAMARKAND!
By the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PP-372 dated September 8, 2022, the project "Modernization of the Water Supply System and Road Network of the City of Samarkand" has been approved for implementation with the financing of the Abu Dhabi Development Fund. As part of this project, LLC "MESMER-EAST" is the General Contractor under Contract No. SAM/ICB-04 for "Construction and Electromechanical Works for Water Supply (Network) Facilities." According to the contract, the company is engaged in the reconstruction of the main water pipeline that runs along Gagarin and Amir Temur streets. Laying pipes on the city streets is an important task to ensure proper infrastructure and comfort for the city residents. During the implementation of the works, the Company takes into account key aspects such as:

- Planning and Coordination: Detailed planning is necessary to determine the pipe laying routes, taking into account the location of existing infrastructure objects such as roads, sidewalks, cables, etc. Coordination with local authorities and other stakeholders is also important to minimize issues and reduce potential negative consequences.

- Use of Modern Technologies and Methods: The application of modern technologies and methods improves the accuracy of pipe laying and reduces the time of work execution. For example, the use of horizontal directional drilling allows pipes to be laid under roads and other obstacles without the need for their destruction.

- Quality of Materials and Work: The selection of high-quality materials and the execution of work in accordance with norms and standards are important factors for the successful pipe laying. This helps prevent leaks, damages, and reduces the risk of accidents.

- Monitoring and Maintenance: After pipe laying, regular monitoring and maintenance are necessary to identify and rectify potential issues or damages. Regular maintenance will extend the service life of the pipeline system and prevent unforeseen breakdowns. Overall, successful pipe laying on the city streets requires careful planning, the use of modern technologies, high-quality materials, and work execution in accordance with norms and standards. This will ensure reliability, safety, and efficiency of the pipeline system for urban infrastructure.
