WHAT WE DO

"Mesmer" in cooperation with its subsidiaries and international partners is well prepared to act as a turn-key integrator firm in rapidly growing domestic, regional and international markets. The range of services include

Project Management


Project Management "MESMER" lodges the management of its subsidiaries and projects with necessary freedom and autonomy commensurate with their responsibilities. Moreover, company managers use the following project management methods:
• Establishing an executive organization for each project.
• Dividing a main contract into subcontractors for supply, installation and commissioning.
• Proper coordinating and control of each subcontractor through careful planning.
• Organizing Quality Assurance to achieve desired results.

Design and Engineering

The engineering subsidiary company closely collaborates with its customers to establish project engineering teams with the optimum combination of skills and expertise. Our main goal is to find the best solution for the project development and satisfy the customer’s all requirements. Consequently, our activities include:
• Preparing, completing and studying the technical documentation for tenders and optimizing the project technical specifications.
• Reviewing designs prepared by subcontractors.
• Designing auxiliary electrical and mechanical equipment and providing instructions for their production, installation and commissioning.

Procurement

Equipment procurement and engineering works are one of the main activities of our Company. To achieve our goals, we collaborate with a well-organized and highly qualified teams of professionals in the oil and gas industry, the engineering and management department and other specialists. Based on practical experience, we are fully responsible for equipment procurement:
• Considering the possibility of manufacturing equipment both inside and outside the country.
• Contract management with local and foreign contractors for the supply and production of equipment to transfer production technology domestically and reduce the use of foreign currency for projects.
• Setting up procurement schedules including MR, TBE, TBA, PO and expediting of chain procurement.

Operation & Maintenance

Ensuring the safety and reliability of the equipment are one of the most essential conditions for equipment operation. To achieve this goal, "MESMER" offers a wide range of operations and maintenance services at all stages of the project, from feasibility studies to FEED, EPC and commercial operations.
"MESMER" provides operation and maintenance services according to customer needs, including preparation for operation, such as planning, system development and training; consulting services, including profit optimization, improved reliability and safety, as well as a full range of services for carrying out and operating various types of maintenance. In addition, through data collection and analysis, it is possible to anticipate possible operational failures that allows us to use the most advanced technologies to improve the efficiency of the equipment.

Financial Support


One of the most important concerns of a EPC CONTRACTOR company is to prepare financial facilities for their projects. This, "Mesmer" performs its financial support as explained below:
• Recognizing and introducing the investor and participating in project financing.
• Compiling financial models for project execution.
• Obtaining financing proposals from local and overseas sources, arranging negotiations and taking necessary measures until the result acquisition.
• Recognizing financing sources with the least cost and the best condition as to guarantee payment duration and repayment of risks.
