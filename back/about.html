At a Glance

"Mesmer" is a multi-disciplinary firm, offering a broad range of engineering and commercial services to clients in international markets. "Mesmer" provides a full range of project services tailored to suit customer needs, from process design through to business development, equipment procurement, construction management and financial support.

"Mesmer" Founded in 2004, PA’s experienced and accredited professionals have successfully completed references within its areas of practice. We deliver innovative one-stop solutions to major project challenges to create added value. "Mesmer" is active in projects in the Energy, Water-Environment and infra markets.

Our main clients include oil companies, Urban operators, Governments, Municipalities companies, International project developers, Insurance companies and Industrial Zone companies.

Mission

Our mission is to remain a leading multidisciplinary company by building permanent relationship with clients all over the world through providing innovative solutions, world-class services and the best quality of product, the greatest efficiency, premium technical expertise, and the maximum value for their investment.

Vision

The core of our vision is to be recognized by our customers as an excellent and reliable EPC contractor in the field of construction services and gain international recognition as a reliable supplier of high-quality equipment.

Code of Ethics

The policy of "MESMER" is compliance with ethical standards in doing business with employees, directors, shareholders, customers, suppliers, as well as with government bodies, media companies and state enterprises. Integrity, honesty and high reputation are among the most important merits of "MESMER". The company earned all above-mentioned merits due to coherent and high-quality work of its employees, representatives, agents, consultants, officials and directors.

Each employee is responsible for adherence to the company's codes of ethics. It should be noted that the social values, laws and regulations of "MESMER" may differ significantly from generally accepted world standards (some provisions of the code differ from those in other countries). However, "MESMER" strongly believes that the basic principles and rules of the Code are valid, relevant and applicable everywhere and are consistent with provisions and statutes of other jurisdictions.

Collaboration with suppliers, partners and customers

Thanks to the ethical standards of "MESMER", suppliers, partners and customers can fully trust highly qualified specialists of our multi-business company. Our suppliers must have ethical values consistent with MESMER's codes of ethics.

Fair Competition

"MESMER" actively participates in fairs and tenders, observing competition rules in the markets. This applies to both competitors, customers and suppliers.

Quality Management

"MESMER" has more than a decade of experience in the design and management of industrial and other construction projects. Thanks to qualified and experienced personnel, as well as compliance with technical standards in accordance with the ISO 9001:2008 international standard and project management standards such as PMBOK, "MESMER" aims to:

• provide high quality services that meet all customers’ requirements
• raise the company position to the level of a reliable EPC contractor at the national and international levels in accordance with the international quality standards;
• ensure compliance with Health, Safety and Environment requirements;
• provide on-going staff training and promote the culture of collective participation to improve the quality of the company's services;
• improve the quality management system by identifying, implementing and monitoring processes, as well as regular evaluation of the company's performance;
• use the newest technologies for the implementation of the company's projects;
• use integrated management systems and modern information technologies for the improvement of the company's activities.

The success of the company depends on the participation and coherent work of its employees in the proper establishment and maintaining quality standards and systems, as well as the constant improvement of all company activities. The management of the company states with certainty that they will provide full support in creating conditions for the achievement of the above-mentioned goals.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Corporate Social Responsibility is defined as a voluntary commitment, including economic, social and environmental criteria and actions in our corporate practice. "MESMER" endeavors to become a leader in corporate citizenship, creating a sustainable future for younger generations. Our environmental commitment is to reduce the global impact of our production processes, which allows both "MESMER" and our customers to work more effectively through improved health, education and environmental protection.

"MESMER" strives to create working conditions that minimize the risk to the health of all employees. We do our best to solve problems related to the environmental and health impacts of our technological processes by reducing waste and emissions, as well as maintaining work conditions to prevent accidents and injuries.

In addition to supporting the development of natural sources, we provide direct social benefits to a large number of people in need. Social responsibilities of "MESMER" include:
• protection of the environment and natural resources.
• promotion of standardization and quality culture.
• supporting communities and social welfare through jobs creation.
• development of a culture of cooperation and partnership.
