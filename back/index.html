Project Management

"Mesmer" entrusts the management of its subsidiaries and projects with the necessary freedom and autonomy commensurate with their responsibilities. Moreover, the company’s top executives are employing project management methods to enable them to properly under take the projects as follows:
1. Establish an executive organization for each project.

Operation and Maintenance (O&M)

Maintaining safety and reliability while maximizing profit are the core goals of plant operation. To realize this, Mesmer East offers wide-ranging O&M (Operation and Maintenance) services for all project stages, from feasibility studies through FEED, EPC and commercial operation.

"Mesmer" provides O&M services in line with the needs of its clients, including (i) operation preparations, such as planning, system development and training; (ii) consulting services on elements including profit optimization, reliability and safety improvement; and (iii) a full spectrum of services for the planning, performance and operation of various types of maintenance. In addition, through the collection and analysis of operating data and other big data it is able to foresee possible operational faults, which enable us to apply the most advanced technologies to maximize plant operability.

Code of Ethics

The policy of "MESMER" is compliance with ethical standards in doing business with employees, directors, shareholders, customers, suppliers, as well as with government bodies, media companies and state enterprises. Integrity, honesty and high reputation are among the most important merits of "MESMER". The company earned all above-mentioned merits due to coherent and high-quality work of its employees, representatives, agents, consultants, officials and directors.

Each employee is responsible for adherence to the company's codes of ethics. It should be noted that the social values, laws and regulations of "MESMER" may differ significantly from generally accepted world standards (some provisions of the code differ from those in other countries). However, "MESMER" strongly believes that the basic principles and rules of the Code are valid, relevant and applicable everywhere and are consistent with provisions and statutes of other jurisdictions. "MESMER" strives to enhance its value and reputation in the eyes of its clients, employees and shareholders by providing high-quality products in the world market.
